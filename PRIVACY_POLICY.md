# Privacy Policy for Chess Whisperer

**Last Updated:** November 2, 2024

## Introduction

Fleming Analytic ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Chess Whisperer mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
Chess Whisperer does not require user accounts or personal information to function. We do not collect:
- Names
- Email addresses
- Phone numbers
- Contact information
- Location data

### Automatically Collected Information

**Game Data:**
- Chess moves and game history (stored temporarily during gameplay)
- AI difficulty preferences
- Game replay preferences (playback speed)
- All game data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers except as needed for gameplay

**Technical Information:**
- The App connects to our backend API at fleminganalytic.com/chess to:
  - Generate AI moves
  - Retrieve master chess games for viewing
  - Validate chess moves
- No personally identifiable information is transmitted
- Session data is temporary and not linked to any user identity

### Third-Party Services

**Google AdMob:**
- We use Google AdMob to display advertisements
- AdMob may collect device information and advertising identifiers
- AdMob's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- You can opt-out of personalized ads in your device settings

**Google Play In-App Purchases:**
- When you purchase "Remove Ads," the transaction is processed by Google Play
- We receive only confirmation of the purchase, not your payment information
- Google Play's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide chess gameplay functionality
- Generate AI chess moves
- Display master chess games
- Show advertisements (if ads not removed)
- Process in-app purchases

## Data Storage and Security

- All game preferences are stored locally on your device
- Backend API connections use HTTPS encryption
- We do not maintain databases of user information
- Ad removal purchase status is stored locally on your device

## Children's Privacy

Chess Whisperer is suitable for all ages (rated Everyone). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App does not require any personal information to use.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Use the App without providing personal information
- Remove the App and all local data at any time
- Opt-out of personalized ads through your device settings
- Request information about data practices by contacting us

## Ads and Monetization

- Free version displays interstitial ads when starting games
- Ads are provided by Google AdMob
- You can remove all ads through a one-time in-app purchase ($2.99)
- Ad removal is permanent and stored on your device

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the App
- Updating the "Last Updated" date

## Data Retention

- Local game data: Retained until you clear app data or uninstall
- Backend session data: Temporary, deleted after game completion
- Purchase history: Maintained by Google Play per their policies

## Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties.

## International Users

Our backend servers are located in the United States. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of information to the United States.
